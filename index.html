Project Overview

Automated collection for product discovery workflows

MySellComb centralizes crawling tasks into a lightweight dashboard that helps collect product, marketplace, and engagement data. The project focuses on repeatable data acquisition, crawl monitoring, and structured outputs that can support product research and market analysis.
